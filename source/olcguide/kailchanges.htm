Compiled rules from "changes" board

  • Don't use the addapply commands to apply perm_spells, use addaffect <spellname> <level>, i.e. addaffect haste 50
  • Immunity to sleep for mobs will render them immune to both the sleep spell and sap attacks
  • 'if class' works for both mprogs and oprogs
  • The dam_noun groups were looked at and listed, you can find them by typing help dam_types or help damtypes
  • Saveover flags can be used on rooms and items. A saveover room will save all items in it over a reboot/crash and a saveover item will stay in the rooms it's in over a reboot/crash
  • Keyrings can be made and are quite much like containers
  • Emily was brought in here, don't tamper with her or try to make her throw quests
  • Oedit delete, medit delete and redit delete were brought it. They all take a vnum as argument (i.e., in medit, delete #) and will permanently delete a room/object/mobile from an area. Note that while it isn't a problem if you delete a room while you're in it, it transfers you to recall (Temple of UL), but since it transfers you to recall (Temple of UL), and doesn’t remove you from "editor mode", it then (naturally, unless you are listed as a Sendar builder) tells you that you have insufficient OLC rights, and boots you out of "editor mode"). It's a lot easier to do it remotely.
  • The 'isquested' check was put in and it only works if the person is in a scrimmage or quested (a must to use for clanhalls, same with the violence check 'violent')
