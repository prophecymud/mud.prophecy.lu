The Builder Port is a very powerful tool granted to mortals deemed worthy
of its power. As such, when a builder cannot be trusted, they lose their
builder status. The rules on BP are for the most part the same as on live,
with a few notable exceptions:

- Aggressive Actions/PK Rules:
These do not apply in part or in whole to BP.
- Swearing
You will not be punished for swearing. However, out of courtesy, keep it down.
- Multiplaying
Due to the fact that builders are 93 and above, we encourage area testing with
level 1-91 mortal chars. Remember: you are an immortal on BP, thus you can
do things you CAN'T do on live.

Rules not found on Live that apply to BP:

- "Horseplay"

BP is a place to work and create new areas for live. It is NOT a chatroom,
a place to test PK skills, or anything which doesn't involve building an area.

- Privacy (Non-Imped areas)

Consider every builder's area to be their home. You must ask before entering,
and leave when they log off. Under no circumstances are you ever to explore
an area under construction. Failure to comply may result in deletion from BP.

- Implemented Areas/Immortal commands

This is the golden rule of BP. Never are you allowed to explore an area on BP,
stat eq, or stat mobs for the purpose of gaining knowledge for use on live. You
MAY, as a builder however, check mobs, eq, and mprogs for knowledge to enhance
your own area. We, the BP Staff, will know the difference. Use common sense
or you'll find out very quickly how much you can be punished for disobeying
this rule. Failure to comply can and will result in deletion on BP AND live.

As always, you are responsibile for knowing the rules of BP. Ignorance is
never an excuse. We've trusted you with the commands and access you have.
Don't screw up.

Refer to 'help standard' to see a list of punishments for violations.
