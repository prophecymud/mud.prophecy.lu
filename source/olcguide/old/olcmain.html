The Prophecy OLC Guide

This is a simple page devoted to the commands and structure of OLC building for Prophecy MUD. The links at the side of the page are to text files on the related OLC topic. Builders, feel free to print them out for your reference.

Questions can always be noted on the builder's port, or catch one of the area masters on the live port. You may also email us at this link if you wish to become a builder or can't get a hold of us on either port.

Let us all use this as a tool to build quality, challenging areas that players will enjoy. Good luck, and let's finish those areas!
