Punishment on BP is simple. One of 4 things will happen:

Scenario 1:
You'll be warned. Heed the warning.

Scenario 2:
You'll be demoted and/or suspended of your builder duties for an appropriate
length of time. This is the least harsh punishment.

Scenario 3:
You'll be permanently relieved of your builder duties. Your areas will be
taken over by other, interested builders, and your BP char will be deleted.

Scenario 4:
You'll be removed from the mud entirely, both live and BP. It's not hard
or uncommon to receive this punishment. Needless to say, this is the most harsh.

In this scenario, you did not heed your warning, and you abused our trust.
Do not expect sympathy, you will receive none.
