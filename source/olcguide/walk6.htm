6)   Analysis of different room descriptions

Bad room descriptions

These descriptions have been taken from stock areas. They are all too short, making their areas look quite empty, and they also have various problems.

Description #1

The holy grove
  You are standing amidst the ancient oaks and poplars in the holy grove. You
feel unusually happy here, as if great burdens have been lifted from Your
shoulders. From here, pleasant-looking paths lead east, west and south.

Analysis

"You are standing amidst the ancient oaks and poplars in the holy grove" could have been reworded to "Ancient oaks and poplars dot the holy grove". "You feel unusually happy" is forced RP, forcing the characters inside the room to feel happy.

Description #2

Main Street
  This was once a magnificent street, but now is in total ruin. The
streets are barren and windswept and the silence is unending. A dark alley
leads north and south. Howls and screams echo through the deserted city.

Analysis

The room description contradicts itself. In the second sentence, it says "the silence is unending", but in the fourth sentence it says "Howls and screams echo through the deserted city".

Description #3

Carnage
  Here, you see a gruesome sight. Blood is everywhere... on the trees and
soaked into the ground. Two carcasses lie in front of you, seeming to look
up at you. The forest is impassible to the south, but the other directions
look ok.

Analysis

"Here, you see a gruesome sight" contains the word "you" in it. Also, elipsis (as in "...") is used in the second sentence. Finally, slang is used in the last sentence, as in "the other directions look ok". "Ok" could have been replaced by "safer".

Average room descriptions

I wrote this description myself, so don't try to find it in any area - it doesn't exist.

Description #4

A pond
   Here is a pond which looks really nice. The water is clear, and the fish
are swimming happily in it. The bright sun shines down, and the pond reflects
the blue, cloudless sky. Around the pond are several houses. You can go north,
east, south and west from here.

Analysis

"Nice" is used too often nowadays, and it is generally a good idea to use other adjectives, such as "inviting" or "magnificent", which have a much more stronger impact on the reader. It is also a bad idea to describe the weather and time ("The bright sun shines down, and the pond reflects the blue, cloudless sky"), since it could easily have been night time, when there's no sun, or it could be raining. "You can go north, east, south and west from here" can be replaced by "The pond can be exited to the north, east, south and west from here". More description of the houses and perhaps what the edge of the pond looks like can be added.

Good room descriptions

The first description has been taken from Urgit's area (Verkat), and the second from Drosta's (Boktor).

Description #5

Northern Path
  This dusty path continues north and south from here, twisting and turning
somewhat to match the contours of the hillside, the bulk of which lies to
the east. This route itself is rather worn as if from many footfalls,
although there doesn't seem to be much of use or interest in this section of
the settlement. To the west is the entrance to a thatched dwelling, and a
scrubbed stone step juts out from its front. Cleaning the step must be a
regular chore considering all the dust flying about here.

Analysis

A good description of the path itself - where the path leads (This dusty path continues north and south from here), what the path looks like (dusty path... twisting and turning to match the contours of the hillside... rather worn as if from many footfalls), how frequented is this path (there doesn't seem to be much of use or interest in this section of the settlement). The surroundings of the path has also been described (hillside, the bulk of which lies to the east... To the west is the entrance to a thatched dwelling, and a scrubbed stone step juts out from its front).

Description #6

The Merchant's Guild
  This is the foyer of the Merchant's Guild building. It is lushly
decorated with fine rugs and tapestries all over and expensive furnishing
all across the room. Merchants are walking in and out, meeting with the
administrator or one of lower position within the guild. There is a large
skylight in the ceiling, showering the room with faint light during the day,
and there are several lanterns on the walls, lighting the room at night.
Southwards is the market square and northwards is a hallway leading further
into the building.

Analysis

Drosta describes the furnishings of the foyer (...fine rugs and tapestries... expensive furnishing), the people frequenting this place (Merchants are walking in and out... administrator or one of lower position within the guild). Also, the lighting of the room has been described, and is suitable for both day and night (large skylight in the ceiling... faint light during the day... lanterns... lightning the room at night). The exits of the room have also been covered by the room description (southwards is the market square and northwards is a hallway...).
