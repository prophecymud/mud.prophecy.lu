Compiled rules from "news" board

Tidyness code:

  • Format all room descs. It looks so much nicer.
  • Long descs, if you use them, should start with a capital and end with a period.

Commands and access:

  • Never, ever, ever, ever use commands you have on this port to try and gain advantages on the Live Port.
  • Never enter other Builders' areas without their permission.
  • Never ever enter areas of currently non-existing Builders.

General stuff:

  • Don't reset pkonly items on mobs. BP will crash.
  • The helpfiles area_Edit, mob_edit, object_edit, room_edit, level_chart and resets are a great help if you have any questions.
  • Multiplaying on here is allowed to test your own area. But only on here.
  • Please format your areas credits to the current credit system (type area on Live - typing it here might d/c you). Also put a sign of some sorts in your area saying who built the area.
  • If you stumble upon any Spanish messages e-mail them, and the command you issues to get it, to me at rpgfreak@hotmail.com
  • Use the min-max command in Aedit. Min-max # #, where # and # are the minimum-maximum level for the Aquest range. A tip would be to keep it near your intended level-range for the area.
  • Healers must have the word healer in their name and must not be higher level than 150. This does not mean that you should make every healer level 150. Clan healers can, of course, be level 200.
  • Please check out the Builder reward helpfile on live (help builder) to see what Builder points are and what you can do with them. This file may change now and then, so look at it regularly.
  • Rooms cannot have higher healing rate than 200/200, and 200/200 rooms should be rare. Same with furniture.
  • Don't create death traps (i.e., non-escapable rooms with negative healing rate or no_recall/no_clanrecall etc.)
  • Don't use vnums from other areas as resets in your own area. This is bad.
  • Please, always put a reasonable cost and weight on items. I'm really tired of seeing steel weapons, and iron shields, weighing 0-1 pounds. Really.
  • Never ever use the update_always act flag on mobs. This will indeed screw things up on Live. Hopefully we'll get around to removing all buggy/non-usable flags soon enough.

By Kail
