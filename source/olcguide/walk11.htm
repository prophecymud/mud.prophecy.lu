We'll now focus on v0-v4.

[v0] Weapon class: exotic

Pretty self-explanatory: what kind of weapon it is. A cudgel is a staff, so we'll need to type "v0 spear". For a quick recap, here's the edited version of the weapons helpfile on live:

axe             axes, ranging from hand to great (but not halberds)
dagger          knives, daggers, and other stabbing weapons
flail           ball-and-chain type weapons
mace            clubs, hammers, maces
polearm         pole weapons (except spears), including halberds
spear           spears and staves, but not polearms
sword           from rapier to claymore
whip            whips, chains, and bullwhips
exotic          strange magical weapons

[v1] Number of dice: [0]
[v2] Type of dice: [0]

A weapon has dam 5d6, which means avg dam 18. Another example is a weapon that has dam dice of 1d75, which means the damage can vary from 1 to 75, and the mean is 38. An easier way of thinking it is rolling dice: v1 is the number of dice, v2 is the sides on each die.

It is not advisable to set a weapon to 40d1, because it will never deal more than 40 dam, whereas a weapon with 16d4 can deal a maximum of 64 dam, although the risk is also dealing the minimum of 4 dam. Please don't make any weapons with a higher avg than 100, even though the points system does not allow this anyway.

[v3] Type: none

v3 determines the dam noun of the weapon, e.g. slice, thwack etc. The list of dam nouns can by viewed by typing "? weapon".

[v4] Special type: none

Setting v4 gives the weapons different flags, namely: flaming, frost, vampiric, sharp, vorpal, twohands, shocking, poison. Each flag does something different everytime the weapon hits the opponent, for example flaming flag will 'burn' the opponent, and may blind with smoke. To set a flag, type "v4 <flag>"
