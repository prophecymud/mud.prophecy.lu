The Prophecy OLC Guide

This page is dedicated to the commands and structure of OLC Building for Prophecy MUD, part of an ongoing effort to provide support and encouragement to our Building Staff in producing quality areas that improve and enhance our world. While some of the files are tailored to the theme and Building structure on Prophecy, we hope it might also provide some valuable assistance to Builders elsewhere in the MUD community.

The links at the side of the page are to files on the related OLC topics. Most detail the commands within OLC used to construct an area, but there are also details on Builder application, rules and guidelines for building on Prophecy, and other pieces of useful information for a Builder. An OLC walkthrough was recently added by Salmissra that provides a detailed step-by-step guide to further aid the new Builder to settle into the task. Technical OLC instructions, style and strategy guides are being contributed by experienced Builders that may well be useful as inspiration and a further reference source.

Feel free to print the pages of this guide for reference. Questions about this guide, problems in Building or suggestions for improvement can be noted to the Area Staff on the Builders' or Live Ports, or you can catch one of us online. Builder Applications from enthusiastic new and experienced Builders alike are always welcome and will be considered promptly.

I hope this guide will continue to be of use to Builders throughout the MUD community, and that with this reference source, and the continuing area code updates we can continue to produce high quality, original, challenging areas for the players of Prophecy to enjoy.
