16) Resets

There are two way of resetting objects and mobiles in a room: one is to do it within redit, while the other is outside of redit. I personally feel outside of redit is less confusing, but of course opinions vary. Having never tried the previous method, I can only explain resets outside of redit.

So to load my shopkeeper in my shop, I will type "reset 1 mob 1000 1 1". This means this is reset number 1, for a mobile of vnum 1000, with a global limit of 1 and an area limit of 1. Then I type "reset" to see what I have done.

reset 1 mob 1000 1 1

Reset added.

reset

You might have noticed that "Ulgo" was cut off". This is pretty normal - there is nothing to be afraid of. This just means the MUD cuts the short description in the middle and the colour tag was left open. This is also why it doesn't look aligned.

Now I will let the shopkeeper wear the balance (vnum 1000) but sell the leaves (vnum 1001).

reset 2 obj 1000 hold

Reset added.

reset 3 obj 1001

Reset added.

reset

By setting the number of the items that load on the shopkeeper after the number of the mobile, the MUD recognises the objects to load on the mobile ("in the inventory of").

Now we type "edit room reset", which loads the mobiles and the objects (except objects on the ground... it's a bit buggy).
