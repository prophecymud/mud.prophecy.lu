? area

- changed: an automatic flag when someone edits anything inside the area.
- added: an area that has been recently added to the mud.
- noquest: no autoquests will be given in the area with this flag.
- noenter: allows nobody but the builder to enter the area.
- newbie: an area that only "newbies" (levels 1-5) can enter, like the mud school. Should not really be used.
- norecall: an area generally made no-recall for purposes of the recall skill, “word of recall” spell, or clan recall.
