12) Object values (v0-v4)

Here is a table of different types of objects, their values:

Light   Money

[v2] Light:  [0]

  

[v0] Gold:   [0]
Used to set the number of hours the light will last. 0 means the light will not work, -1 or 999 means the light will last forever. The amount of silver the money is worth when picked up.
     
Wand/staff   Scroll/potion/pill

[v0] Level:          [0]
[v1] Charges Total:  [0]
[v2] Charges Left:   [0]
[v3] Spell:          reserved

  

[v0] Level:  [0]
[v1] Spell:  reserved
[v2] Spell:  reserved
[v3] Spell:  reserved
[v4] Spell:  reserved
v0 - the level of spells inside the wand/staff
v1 - the total number of charges the wand/staff holds"
v2 - the number of charges in wand/staff when loaded (i.e. may not be at total)
v3 - the spell the wand/staff casts		 v0 - the level of the scroll determines the level of the spells
v1-v4 - slots for different spells
     
Armour   Weapon

[v0] Ac pierce       [0]
[v1] Ac bash         [0]
[v2] Ac slash        [0]
[v3] Ac exotic       [0]

  

[v0] Weapon class:   exotic
[v1] Number of dice: [0]
[v2] Type of dice:   [0]
[v3] Type:           none
[v4] Special type:   none
v0-v3 - the base ac of the eq, positive integer v0 - determines type of weapon
v1 - the amount of damage dice rolled
v2 - the number of faces on the damage dice (does not have to be physically possible)
v3 - determines dam noun of weapon
v4 - determines weapon flags
     
Container/keyring   Furniture

[v0] Weight:     [0 kg]
[v1] Flags:      [none]
[v2] Key:     none [0]
[v3] Capacity    [0]
[v4] Weight Mult [0]

  

[v0] Max people:     [0]
[v1] Max weight:     [0]
[v2] Furniture Flags: none
[v3] Heal bonus:     [0]
[v4] Mana bonus:     [0]
v0 - the weight the container/keyring can hold
v1 - determines whether it can be closed/locked etc.
v2 - key of the object if any
v3, v4 - not used		 v0 - the maximum number of people who can sit on the furniture
v1 - the maximum weight the furniture can support
v2 - determines positions players can make on furniture (sleep, sit etc.) and prepositions
v3, v4 - the healing rate of furniture
     
Food   Drink container

[v0] Food hours: [0]
[v1] Full hours: [0]
[v3] Poisoned:   No

  

[v0] Liquid Total: [0]
[v1] Liquid Left:  [0]
[v2] Liquid:       water
[v3] Poisoned:     No
v0 - number of food hours (ticks) food gives
v1 - not used, appears to have no effect
v3 - determines if food is poisoned or not		 v0 - the total amount of liquid the container can hold
v1 - the amount of liquid in container when it loads
v2 - the type of liquid loading in container
v3 - determines if liquid is poisoned or not 
     
Portal   Fountain

[v0] Charges:        [0]
[v1] Exit Flags:     none
[v2] Portal Flags:   none
[v3] Goes to (vnum): [0]

  

[v0] Liquid Total: [0]
[v1] Liquid Left:  [0]
[v2] Liquid:       water
v0 - the number of times the portal can be used. -1 means the portal lasts forever
v1 - although it can be set, it has no effect on the portal, so don't use
v2 - behaviour of portal, e.g. buggy means portal may not work 100% of the time
v3 - destination of portal		 v0, v1 - not set, as a fountain contains a neverending supply of liquid
v2 - the type of liquid held in the fountain

The following object types do not have any values: trash, treasure, key, boat, npccorpse, protect, map, warpstone, roomkey, gem, jewelry, jukebox
