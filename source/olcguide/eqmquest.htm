File for miniquests

Miniquest eq that receives a point bonus must be the reward of
the miniquest and not a tool of it. In other words, the piece
of eq you get at the completion of the quest gets the bonus, not
every piece of eq that is a part of the quest.

If the piece of eq at the end of a miniquest is level 91 to use
then you may up the cap on a single mod by 1 points worth. In
other words, a piece of eq, level 91 to use, normally has a
hit/dam cap of 5/5. If the piece however is the product of a
miniquest (IMM approval required) the cap may be moved to 6/6.
This only applies to the normal attributes.
